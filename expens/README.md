# Medical & Travel Expense Tracker

A simple application to track, organize, and manage medical and travel-related expenses. Keep records of receipts, categorize spending, and generate reports for reimbursement, tax filing, or personal budgeting.

## Features

- 📋 Log medical expenses (doctor visits, medications, insurance claims, etc.)
- ✈️ Log travel expenses (flights, hotels, transport, meals, etc.)
- 🗂️ Categorize expenses by type, date, and purpose
- 🧾 Upload and attach receipts/invoices
- 📊 Generate summary reports (monthly, yearly, by category)
- 💰 Track reimbursement status
- 🔍 Search and filter expense history
